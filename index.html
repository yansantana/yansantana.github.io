About Us

BlueFile Immigration Services Inc. is a Toronto Canada based Immigration Consulting Company

Our Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC), Rey “Charlie” Collantes, Managing Director, is a registered and a licensed member of the Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council (ICCRC) in good standing..

  • Our Mission is Simple

    To be able to make your immigration process done Right from the Start utilizing our knowledge, skills, expertise, and professional network.

  • Our Goal is Specific

    To assist, guide and support you and your family in finding the Right immigration program appropriate to your qualifications throughout the process in achieving a high rate of success from the moment you decide to immigrate until you and your family become Canadian citizens.

Your Dream and Aspiration to succeed in life will be our motivating factor to excel in delivering our services to help you Visit, Work, Study, and/or Live in Canada permanently.
